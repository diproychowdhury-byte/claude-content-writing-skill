Content writers are often pulled into writing/reviewing microcopy across dozens of product surfaces with short turn around time.

Empty states, error messages, confirmation popups, button labels, etc.
Important? Yes.
The best use of their time? Not always

I have built a Claude "content writing skill" specifically for generating consistent and high quality microcopy based on IBM Carbon and Google Material design system's guidelines. The goal of this is to free Content Writers to work on higher-impact work and enable PMs and Product designers to create decent microcopy with content support isn't available.

Just upload the skill.md file into your Claude account and then paste a mockup or write a prompt to get copy recommendations, with a mockup based on the guidelines.
